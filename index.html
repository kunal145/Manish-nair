Employability

In the realm of employment, a single word that gained physical existence only in 2021, challenges have always been prevalent in our country. India, known as the youth capital, boasts the largest captive audience of eager learners and torchbearers of modern India. Recognizing this immense potential, we embarked on a journey to create a book on "EMPLOYABILITY." Our goal is to assess opportunities against the gold standards and bridge the gap between industry expectations and the lack of appropriate resources. We critically examined the enhancement of learners' quality and aimed to equip them for higher packages and career aspirations. By benchmarking against the evolving job market, we endeavored to provide the best preparatory tool in the form of this book. Our aim is to guide today's youth and bring them closer to the vision of a "skill India," both on a personal level and for our nation.
